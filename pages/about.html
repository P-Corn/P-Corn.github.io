Stellerium is a massive open world multiplayer experience that can be played across all platforms. It is an RPG survival game that tests your cooperation skills as you survive in a world full of creatures and other players.

  • Settle down and protect your assets by building a base
  • Explore the universe to uncover hidden secrets
  • Choose a skill tree and strengthen your abilities
  • unleash your creativity with the sandbox crafting system
